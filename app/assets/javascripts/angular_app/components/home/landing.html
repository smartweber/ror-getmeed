HOW DOES IT WORK?

IN A NUT SHELL

Students typically don’t have a huge amount of professional experience, but have demonstrated their skills and capabilities in and outside the classroom. On Meed students are able to use all their coursework, independent projects, and extracurriculars to build their professional identity. By participating in communities, students are able to show their interest and expertise in a given field.

For example: Sharing knowledge and answering questions in a community builds your professional brand.

SO WHAT IS MEED?

YOUR PROFESSIONAL COMMUNITY

INTRODUCTION

Meed’s goal is to help students build detailed, engaging online resumes — whether they are experienced postgraduate students or freshmen navigating their first semester. Students simply create a profile that includes their past experience, their education and classes, a portfolio of projects they’ve completed, and thoughts on their favorite career-related topics. Once completing their profile, students enter a community of alumni, recruiters, industry leaders, and their peers, dedicated to launching their career.
